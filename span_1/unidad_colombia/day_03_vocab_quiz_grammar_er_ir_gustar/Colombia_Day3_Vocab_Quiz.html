Vocabulary Quiz — Unidad Colombia / Spanish 1
Instrucciones: Section A — Match each Spanish term to its English meaning (write the letter). Section B — Fill in the blank with a word from the word bank (use each word once). Section C — Answer in Spanish. No talking during the quiz.
Section A — Matching (10 pts)

Write the letter of the correct English meaning next to each number. Each letter is used once. Teacher: adjust terms to match Day 2 word list.

1.la plaza
2.el colegio
3.el mercado
4.la clase
5.el libro
6.la mochila
7.la ventana
8.la puerta
9.la mesa

Options (use each once):
A. the plaza/square   B. the school   C. the market   D. the class   E. the book   F. the backpack   G. the window   H. the door   I. the table   J. the pencil

Complete each sentence with a word from the word bank. Use each word once.

1. En Bogotá la de Bolívar es famosa.

Answer in Spanish.
