Spanish 1 — Colombia Unit Exam
Listening + reading + grammar + writing
Section 1 — Listening
Directions

Your teacher will read short statements and mini-dialogues. Answer in English unless told otherwise.

1. What city does the speaker mention?
2. True or false: The student likes to study in the library.
3. Where is the backpack?
4. How does the speaker feel?
5. What is the speaker going to do after school?
Section 2 — Reading
Laura vive en Cartagena. Le gusta bailar y comer arepas con su familia. Después de la escuela, va a la biblioteca porque tiene que estudiar. Hoy está contenta porque su clase aprende sobre Colombia y su música. En su mochila tiene un libro y un cuaderno.
1. Where does Laura live?
2. Name two things Laura likes or does.
3. Why does she go to the library?

Grammar and writing
Section 3 — Grammar
1. Yo ____ café. (beber)
2. Nosotros ____ en Medellín. (vivir)
3. A mí me gusta ____ español.
4. La mochila ____ al lado de la silla. (estar)
5. Yo voy a ____ después de la escuela.
Section 4 — Writing
Write 5-6 sentences about a student in Colombia. Include:
  • one location sentence with estar
  • one sentence with gustar
  • one sentence with ir
  • at least one -ER or -IR verb
